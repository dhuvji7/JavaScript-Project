Bat Ball Stump Game

🎮 How the Game Works

This is a simple cricket-themed game where you and the computer both choose one of the three options: Bat, Ball, or Stump.

The result is based on a custom rule set:

  • Bat 🏏 vs Ball ⚾ → Bat wins
  • Ball ⚾ vs Stump 💥 → Ball wins
  • Bat 🏏 vs Stump 💥 → Stump wins
  • Same choice → It's a tie

The computer picks randomly. Your goal is to choose wisely based on possible outcomes!
